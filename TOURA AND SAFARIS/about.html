about us

We are a registered company with the Government of Kenya and the Ministry of Tourism as a Tours and Travel Company in Nairobi, Kenya. We undertake all types of Luxury & Camping Safari Tours in Kenya and Tanzania which among others are trips to the Masai Mara, Lake Nakuru, Amboseli, Mt. Kenya, Samburu, Tsavo East and West and to our beaches in Mombasa, Malindi and Lamu.We also do Trekking Safaris, Mountain Climbing in Mt Kenya and Kilimanjaro. Incentive groups are also catered for like Schools, Colleges and Educational Institutions at a discounted rate. We have a list of planned itineraries for different tours spreading all over Kenya and Tanzania. In the event of a client not fitting in any of our itineraries, we are always happy to `tailor make one that will suit the client
